My Story:

i started building in 5th grade to make my own web-based video game with javascript. then, i built my first website with html and css for a history project, hosted on github pages. i learned more about object-oriented programming when making a console version of pokemon red.

i discovered AI in 7th grade. my first project was a simple face finder using opencv. from there, i build a virtual spacebar using hand recognition so i could control youtube videos while eating. as a kid, i continued to tinker, from making an RL-based 3D autonomous car, to destroying web games with computer vision, training a yolo model for drowsy driver detection, and even automating valorant gameplay with a yolo-based pipeline.

these days, i'm focused on applied and theoretical ML research, optimizing and deploying AI at startups, contributing to open-source, and teaching AI to the next generation as an instructor at AI Camp.

what drives me

i'm driven by curiosity and a desire to learn. i believe that every challenge is an opportunity for growth, and i'm always seeking out new knowledge and experiences to expand my understanding. i believe in a transhumanist future where technology helps us transcend our limitations.

my philosophy

i believe that elegant solutions come from constraints and not from unlimited resources. from my own experience, i've learned that the best breakthroughs happen when you're forced to think creatively about efficiency, scalability, and real-world limitations. this becomes clear when you look at things like DeepSeek.

when i'm not coding

when i'm not deep in code or papers, i like to keep my mind engaged in different ways. i love music. in particular, my favorites include denzel curry, j. cole, jid, and joey bada$$. i also listen to podcasts. my favorite podcasts include BG2, My First Million, Freakonomics, (the old) All-In Pod, and Scott Galloway. my favorite forms of entertainment are shonen / seinen anime and manga. my favorite way to stay connected with friends is through gaming, where i really enjoy co-op and party games.
